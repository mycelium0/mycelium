About

Hello, and welcome. This is a small corner of the web where I keep notes on the things I am reading, a few photographs from walks around the neighbourhood, and the odd thought worth writing down. Nothing fancy — just a quiet personal page that I update when I have a spare afternoon.

I grew up near the coast and still spend most weekends outdoors with a camera. When I am not out walking, I am usually re-reading old paperbacks or tending a stubborn tomato plant on the balcony.
